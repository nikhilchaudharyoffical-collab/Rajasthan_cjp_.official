Our Movement's Vision.

Hum yahan naye sarkari mehal banane ya tax payers ke paise par aish karne nahi aaye hain. Hum yahan baar-baar, likhit mein, aur bohot loudly poochhne aaye hain ki— Humare Government Exams Ke Papers Leak Kaise Ho Rhe Hain? Aur unka zimmedar kaun hai? That's it.

Chapter Two

The Rajasthan Manifesto.

01
Universal High-Speed Digital Infrastructure Act (Tech Demand) Rajasthan ke har ek gaon aur shahar ke youth ke liye digital education aur self-employment ko scale karne ke liye, pure state mein minimum 200 Mbps free internet network infrastructure mandatory kiya jaye. Paper leak ya testing ke naam par baar-baar internet ban (shutdown) karne ki jagah, modern tech security layers lagakar digital ecosystem ko uninterrupted rakha jaye.
02
Chunav jeetne ke baad dal badalne wale kisi bhi MP/MLA par agle 20 saal tak public office hold karne aur election ladne par mukammal ban lagega.
03
Mahilao ko Vidhan Sabha aur Cabinet mein poora 50% reservation diya jayega, bina kisi kintu-parantu ke.
04
State Portal Modernization & UI/UX Standards (Tech Demand) Sarkari job portals, admit card sites aur recruitment websites ke servers aur UI/UX ko completely modern aur seamless banaya jaye. Form bharte waqt servers crash hona ya payment stuck hona poori tarah band ho, taaki rural areas ke students ka sasta internet data aur time waste na ho. Har website par responsive aur immediate AI-driven support helpline active ho.
05
Truly independent media ko space dene ke liye, bade corporate houses dwara owned media outlets ke state licenses par kadi janch hogi.
