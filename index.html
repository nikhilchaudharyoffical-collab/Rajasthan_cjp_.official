Chapter Two

The Rajasthan Manifesto.

01
Agar state mein koi bhi sarkari exam ka paper leak hua, toh bina kisi delay ke Education Minister ko turant pad se sakh (sack) kiya jayega.
02
Chunav jeetne ke baad dal badalne wale kisi bhi MP/MLA par agle 20 saal tak public office hold karne aur election ladne par mukammal ban lagega.
03
Mahilao ko Vidhan Sabha aur Cabinet mein poora 50% reservation diya jayega, bina kisi kintu-parantu ke.
04
Berozgaari Bhatta (Allowance) ke liye chakkar kaatna band. Chronically online youth ke khate mein direct aur automatic transfer system hoga.
05
Truly independent media ko space dene ke liye, bade corporate houses dwara owned media outlets ke state licenses par kadi janch hogi.
